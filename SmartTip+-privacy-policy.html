SmartTip+ Privacy Policy

Last Updated: September 24, 2024
Effective Date: September 24, 2024

1. Introduction

Calvin Studio ("we," "us," or "our") operates the SmartTip+ mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

SmartTip+ is a tip calculation app that helps users calculate tips using receipt scanning, service quality ratings, and customizable percentage calculations.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Service Quality Ratings: Star ratings you provide for service quality assessment
  • Custom Tip Percentages: Your preferred tip calculation settings
  • Saved Calculations: Tip calculations you choose to save locally on your device

2.2 Information Automatically Collected

  • Camera Images: Receipt photos captured for text recognition (processed locally and immediately deleted)
  • Location Information: Approximate location for regional tip customs (optional, requires your consent)
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous app usage statistics to improve our service
  • Device Information: Device type, OS version, and app version for compatibility

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Core Functionality: Processing receipt images for automatic tip calculation
  • Service Enhancement: Providing region-specific tip suggestions
  • Data Storage: Saving your calculation history locally
  • App Improvement: Analyzing usage patterns
  • Technical Support: Diagnosing and resolving issues

4. Camera Permission

4.1 Purpose

  • Capture receipt images for OCR
  • Extract text for tip calculation
  • Enable quick and accurate bill detection

4.2 Data Processing

All image processing is local; images are deleted immediately after extraction. No images are stored or transmitted.

4.3 User Control

You can grant, deny, or revoke camera permission anytime.

5. Location Information

5.1 Purpose

Used for region-appropriate tip suggestions and local customs.

5.2 Data Handling

Approximate location only, no precise coordinates stored. Can be disabled anytime.

6. Advertising Services

6.1 Google AdMob

We use AdMob for ads. Google may collect device identifiers and usage data. See Google Privacy Policy for details.

6.2 User Control

Opt-out options available in iOS and Android settings.

6.3 Third-party Privacy Policies

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob Privacy Information

7. Data Storage and Security

Data stored locally, encrypted by device. No cloud storage. Receipt images deleted after processing.

8. Data Sharing and Disclosure

No data sales. Limited sharing only with AdMob, legal requirements, or anonymous analytics providers.

9. Your Rights and Choices

You can view, delete, or export data. Permissions (camera, location, notifications) fully controllable.

10. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

11. International Users

All processing is local to your device. Local laws may apply.

12. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this policy. Changes notified in-app. Last Modified date updated.

13. Contact Information

14. Legal Compliance

Complies with CCPA, GDPR, COPPA, and other applicable regulations.

15. Conclusion

SmartTip+ is designed with privacy in mind. We minimize collection, process locally, and give you control.
